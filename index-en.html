Hi, I'm Abby!

I'm a budding front-end developer who enjoys creating websites from scratch. I love the satisfaction of bringing a design to life, especially when it involves layout tuning or animation effects — polishing things to make them smoother, cleaner, and more enjoyable for users is what makes the work meaningful for me.

I care about intuitive UI and smooth UX. Whether it’s button feedback, layout alignment, or mobile responsiveness, I aim to make things clean and easy to use. I also enjoy learning by doing — whenever I see cool effects or design inspiration online, I try them out myself and slowly discover my own style and rhythm.

I'm currently still practicing and building my portfolio. I hope to keep improving through real projects and challenges. Feel free to explore my works — and if you’d like to connect or chat, I’d be happy to talk!
